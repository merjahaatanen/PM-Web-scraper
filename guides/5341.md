# Operator Troubleshooting Checklist: General Maintenance

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptoms | Troubleshooting Steps | When to Call Maintenance |
| :---------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Scrap Material Management | - Excess scrap material accumulating in yard or designated outdoor areas. | - Visually check if scrap material bins or designated outdoor areas are full.<br>- Notify your supervisor or maintenance when scrap material is nearing full capacity. | - When scrap material accumulation is excessive and requires hauling away. |
| Rack & Storage Issues | - Rack is bent or damaged.<br>- Upright on rack is damaged.<br>- Heavy pallet on a damaged rack section.<br>- Workstation or rack moved from its original location. | - Visually inspect racks for any bent, dented, or broken components (uprights, beams, screens).<br>- Immediately offload any material from sections of racks that appear damaged.<br>- Do NOT load material onto damaged racks.<br>- Do NOT attempt to move heavy racks or workstations yourself. | - Immediately if you observe any bent, broken, or damaged rack components (uprights, beams, screens).<br>- If a rack or workstation needs to be moved or repositioned. |
| Shop Tools & Aids Malfunction | - Strapping tool not working, missing parts, or cutter too far down.<br>- Manual clamp not working, visible damage (e.g., dropped).<br>- Material kart side broken or detached. | - For strapping tools, check for any visible missing parts or if the cutter blade appears misaligned.<br>- For manual clamps, inspect for signs of physical damage (e.g., impact marks).<br>- For material karts, check if side panels are loose or detached. | - If strapping tools are not working, have missing parts, or the cutter needs adjustment.<br>- If a manual clamp is broken or not functioning correctly.<br>- If a material kart side is broken or detached. |
| Desk & Furniture Needs | - New tables or desks are unassembled.<br>- Keyboard track has sharp edges or a missing protection cover. | - Inspect keyboard trays and tracks for sharp edges or missing safety covers. | - For assembly of new tables, desks, or other shop furniture.<br>- If a keyboard track has sharp edges, a missing cover, or needs a new tray. |
| Facility Door & Electrical Needs | - Power strip loose on table.<br>- Office door closer not working; door slamming.<br>- Roll-up door hit, stuck halfway, not moving up/down. | - Visually check if power strips are securely fastened to surfaces; if loose, reposition safely to prevent tripping hazards.<br>- Do NOT use power strips that are damaged or have exposed wires.<br>- Observe door closing speed (is it slamming shut or closing too slowly?).<br>- If a roll-up door is stuck or damaged, immediately stop operations near it; do NOT attempt to force the door. | - If a power strip is loose and cannot be safely secured, or for installation of new power outlets/trees.<br>- If a door closer is not working or the door is slamming.<br>- Immediately if a roll-up door is struck, stuck, or not operating correctly. |
| Facility Lighting & Floor Needs | - Light obstructs operations (e.g., above new equipment).<br>- Need more floor mats in an area. | - Observe if overhead lighting interferes with operations or new equipment.<br>- Assess if additional floor mats are needed for safety or comfort in high-traffic or standing areas. | - To request repositioning or modification of lights.<br>- To request additional floor mats for placement. |
| Overhead Support Damage | - Support chain for roll foam ripped down, roll foam not secured. | - Immediately clear the area around any fallen or damaged overhead supports.<br>- Do NOT attempt to reattach any support chains. | - Immediately if any overhead support chains are ripped down, broken, or damaged. |
| Pneumatic Connections | - Air connection popped off, air leak. | - Listen for hissing sounds indicating an air leak.<br>- Visually inspect air connections for dislodgement or damage. | - Immediately if an air connection pops off, or you hear or see an air leak. |
| Divider System Repair | - Plastic laminate delivery divider out of track, top stopper broken. | - Inspect dividers to ensure they are properly seated in their tracks.<br>- Check if any stoppers are missing or broken. | - If a divider is out of its track or a stopper is broken or missing. |
