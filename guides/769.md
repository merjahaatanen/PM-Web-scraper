# Operator Troubleshooting Checklist: Vacuum Lift Holz-Her Saw

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptoms | Troubleshooting Steps | When to Call Maintenance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Vacuum Suction Loss | - Not picking up sheets properly<br>- Hissing sound heard during lift<br>- Slow to lift material | - Check for sheets sticking together<br>- Inspect vacuum cups for debris or misalignment<br>- Ensure cup is sealing properly against the material | - Cup is torn or damaged<br>- Cup is loose and cannot be tightened<br>- Suction failure persists after cleaning |
| Structural Damage | - Handle has come loose<br>- Assembly arm and body separating | - Visually inspect brackets for cracks or breaks | - Brackets are broken<br>- Handle is detached<br>- L-brackets require replacement |

| Issue | Related Work Order #s | Parts Often Needed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Vacuum Suction Loss | 12210, 16971, 17119 | None operator-replaceable |
| Structural Damage | 4181 (Specialist repair), 18506 (Specialist repair) | None operator-replaceable |
