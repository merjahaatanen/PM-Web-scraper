# Operator Troubleshooting Checklist: TPF

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptoms | Troubleshooting Steps | When to Call Maintenance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Air/Vacuum Hose Leaks or Damage | - Hissing sound or visible air escaping from a hose<br>- Reduced air pressure to a tool or station<br>- Hose appears torn, cut, or disconnected<br>- Vacuum hose not picking up debris or spraying dust<br>- Air nozzle has come undone<br>- Hose line is visibly leaking | - Visually inspect all accessible air and vacuum hoses for cuts, tears, or loose connections.<br>- Listen for hissing sounds to pinpoint air leaks.<br>- If an air nozzle is disconnected, attempt to securely reattach it. | - Hose is torn, cut, or damaged and cannot be reconnected.<br>- Leak persists after checking connections.<br>- Air or vacuum system performance is still poor after basic checks.<br>- Hose needs to be replaced.<br>- Air nozzle is broken or cannot be reattached. |
| Glue System & Valve Operation Issues | - Glue gun continues to pass glue even when handle is released<br>- Glue drain nozzle is stuck or not draining properly<br>- Valve on a barrel (e.g., blue barrel) is broken or not operating | - For a glue gun that won't stop: Carefully clean around the return spring actuator knob and try to adjust it.<br>- For glue draining issues: Attempt to clean the PVC valve and flush with water to remove excess glue.<br>- Ensure the valve is fully open or fully closed when operating to prevent glue drying in the mechanism.<br>- Ensure the water container for the spray gun is changed daily to prevent contamination. | - Glue gun still malfunctions after cleaning/adjusting.<br>- Glue drain nozzle/valve remains stuck after cleaning/flushing.<br>- A valve on a barrel is visibly broken.<br>- Glue system is leaking. |
| General Tool Malfunctions | - Foam roll chain in CNC has come down<br>- Nail gun has a nail stuck<br>- Hand buffer is not working (e.g., dead, intermittent power) | - If the foam roll chain is down, carefully rehang it.<br>- If the nail gun has a stuck nail, follow safe procedures to clear the jammed nail.<br>- For hand buffer issues, check if it's plugged in correctly. | - Foam roll chain cannot be rehung or is damaged.<br>- Nail gun remains jammed or does not operate after clearing a stuck nail.<br>- Hand buffer shows no power, a broken wire, or internal issues.<br>- Any other tool malfunction that isn't a simple adjustment or jam clearance. |
| HPL Ganonmat Program/Alignment Issues | - Machine program seems off (e.g., 1/16th on bottom hinge)<br>- Measurements are consistently off, even if within tolerance, and you want to "dial in" accuracy<br>- Parts are not being drilled or machined accurately | - Verify current measurements of affected parts.<br>- Document the specific deviation or offset observed (e.g., "1/16th on the bottom hinge").<br>- Collect any affected parts for inspection by Maintenance. | - Any reported program offset or alignment issue that affects part quality.<br>- When parts are consistently out of specification or require adjustment.<br>- If the Y-axis appears to be off. |
