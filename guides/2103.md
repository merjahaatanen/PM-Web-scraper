# Operator Troubleshooting Checklist: Flamex spark detection and extinguishing system

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptoms | Troubleshooting Steps | When to Call Maintenance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Water Leak from Extinguishing Assembly | - Water on the floor around the Flamex extinguishing assembly<br>- Visible drip or spray from the extinguishing assembly components | - Visually inspect the extinguishing assembly for the exact source of the leak.<br>- Note if the leak is a drip or a steady stream.<br>- Observe if the leak is coming from a pipe, valve, or other component. | - Immediately if you observe any water leaking from the Flamex system.<br>- If the leak source is not immediately obvious or you cannot safely inspect it.<br>- If the leak appears to be worsening or cannot be contained. |
